✨ About Me ✨

You know that old joke about two young fish swimming in the ocean. An older fish passes by and says “Morning, boys. How’s the water?” And the two young fish swim on for a bit, and then eventually one of them looks over at the other and goes “What the hell is water?”

I relate to that young fish more than I've related to anything in my life, and I think that informs a lot about how I view and my goals for this class and for my education in general. I want to be able to think critically about the spaces that I occupy and how those spaces can be hostile to others. I want to figure out what I'm swimming in.
